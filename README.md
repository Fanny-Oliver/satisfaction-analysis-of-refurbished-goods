# Apple Refurbished Products Customer Satisfaction Analysis

This project investigates customer satisfaction with refurbished Apple products through a mix of data analysis, customer review mining, and business insight generation. The objective was to understand whether customers are truly satisfied with renewed Apple devices, what factors influence satisfaction the most, and how sellers can improve the refurbished product experience.

Key Highlights
- Measured customer satisfaction using CSAT
- Compared refurbished pricing vs. market pricing
- Analyzed open-text reviews to identify recurring themes
- Identified functionality issues as the main source of dissatisfaction
- Proposed quality control recommendations to improve customer experience

Main Insight

- Customers are generally very satisfied with refurbished Apple products, particularly because they offer strong value for money and often arrive in excellent physical condition. However, battery, speaker, and hardware functionality issues remain the biggest opportunity for improvement.
